Data Deletion Instructions

Vidly respects your privacy and your right to control your personal data. This page explains how you can request deletion of your data collected through Facebook Login.

How to Request Data Deletion

  1. Send an email to:
    hemalmvaghela@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line:
    "Facebook Data Deletion Request"
  3. In the email body, please include:
    • Your full name
    • Your email address used to log in
    • Your Facebook User ID (optional but helpful)

What Happens Next

Once we receive your request:

  • We will verify your identity
  • Your personal data associated with your account will be permanently deleted
  • You will receive a confirmation email

Processing Time

We aim to process all valid data deletion requests within 7 business days.

Data That May Be Retained

Certain information may be retained if required by law or for legitimate business purposes, such as fraud prevention or legal compliance.
